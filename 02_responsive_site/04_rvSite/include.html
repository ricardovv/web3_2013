- - - - - - - - - Solution - - - - - - - - -

- You can have the same element in multiple pages.

- You need to call just one file.

- - - - - - - - - Include - - - - - - - - -

< ?php include("menu.php"); ? >

Include other elements dynamically.
Receip: 1- Have menu (menu.php).
2- put elements on the page.
3- Page to put the include (home.php).
4- put the include on your page.
5- put on the server.
6- test.
